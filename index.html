About Me

I'm a Ph.D. candidate in Chemical and Biological Engineering. A motivated, innovative, problemsolving research professional with several years of experience in characterization of materials such as semiconductor heterostructures and photovoltaic absorbers. Highly skilled in electrical, optical, and morphological characterization techniques. Goal-oriented, self-starter, team player, and able to adapt quickly.
